Streamlining Conversion Paths for Retirement Services

See Prototype

Overview

ValuTeachers, a retirement services company specializing in educator and school employee retirement planning, needed a complete website redesign. The existing site had unclear navigation, outdated aesthetics, and a conversion path that wasn't effectively guiding users toward key actions. My role was to create a modern, intuitive interface that would improve user flow and increase conversions while better communicating their comprehensive retirement services.

Challenge

The primary goals for this redesign were:

Increase conversions by creating clearer paths to action

Improve user flow to help visitors navigate the site intuitively

Modernize the aestheti to align with current web standards

Better communicate services to help users understand ValuTeachers' comprehensive offerings

Without conducting formal user research, I relied on competitor analysis, the client's vision for their new direction, and established UX principles to guide the redesign. The focus was on creating an intuitive path from first visit to conversion while ensuring the diverse range of retirement services was clearly presented.

My Approach

I began with low-fidelity wireframes to establish the information architecture and basic layout structure. This helped me quickly iterate on navigation patterns and content hierarchy without getting caught up in visual details. From here it was a quick progression to medium-fidelity wireframes. These frames introduced more refined layouts, clearer content blocks, and initial thoughts on component placement. The med-fi stage was particularly important for client presentations, as it allowed stakeholders to review the direction before copy and final styling were applied.

Improved Visual Hierarchys

I implemented a clear typographic hierarchy using size, weight, and color to guide users' eyes to the most important content first. Strategic use of whitespace prevents the complex subject matter from feeling overwhelming, while card-based layouts help chunk information into digestible sections.

Clearer Service Communications

ValuTeachers offers a wide range of retirement solutions that can be confusing for someone unfamiliar with financial planning. I designed service pages with consistent layouts that follow a pattern: headline explaining what it is, benefits, who it's for, and a clear next step. This consistency helps users compare options while the structured format reduces cognitive load when processing complex financial information.

Final Deliverables and Prototype

The completed project included:

    Complete low, medium, and high-fidelity wireframes for all key pages
    Interactive prototype demonstrating user flows and conversion paths
    Component library with reusable UI elements
    Style guide documenting typography, colors, spacing, and design patterns
    Responsive designs optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile experiences

Prototype: Iterations tested with families for clarity and usability.

Reflection

This project reinforced the importance of clear user flows in conversion-focused design. By prioritizing ease of navigation and reducing friction at every step, the redesigned site provides users with a straightforward path to understanding ValuTeachers' services and taking action.

If given the opportunity to extend this project, I would love to conduct user testing to validate the conversion path assumptions and identify areas for further optimization. Additionally, building out a more robust design system would support ValuTeachers' growth as they continue to expand their digital presence.
