Overview

A B2C mobile application that directly links students with study abroad advisors and prepares them for what comes ahead with personalized guidance and support.

Role

Product Designer

Team

Internal: Me

External: Art Director

Duration

3 Months

Challenges

With the goal of crafting an immersive and user-centric study abroad ecosystem, Uspire aims to create seamless digital experiences for students pursuing their academic goals worldwide.

Problems

Many students face challenges when seeking advisors who can provide tailored guidance for their unique circumstances while preparing for standardized exams.

Additionally, students frequently encounter difficulties in locating advisors who can offer specialized support based on their individual needs. This often results in a lack of personalized guidance crucial for their exam preparation.

Goals

The team envisioned this product as a comprehensive platform where counselors and advisors can efficiently oversee and guide their students on personalized journeys to success. The team worked together to design the account creation, login, storefront, dashboard, and client request/ offer screens to provide a transparent environment for managing resources and assessing each student's unique strengths and weaknesses.

This resulted in a powerful tool that empowers counselors to effectively support and mentor their students, leading them toward academic achievement and fulfilling their dreams.

Understanding the Client's Mission

Our client engagement involved thorough questioning to ensure a precise understanding of their vision. Upon receiving comprehensive responses, the project gained clarity, igniting our enthusiasm for the design process with the following key insights from our discussions:

Minimalist and Simple Design

The client's strong emphasis on minimalism and simplicity in design was evident. They provided us with brands that served as inspiration, allowing us to align our creative direction with their preferences.

Advisor-Student fit

Our client stressed the importance of connecting students with the right advisors for their study abroad journey, emphasizing personalized support.

Comprehensive Exam Prep

Beyond exam readiness, the client aimed to provide students with a holistic approach to academic success, covering study strategies and support.

Competitive Analysis

To gain a better understanding of similar, existing sites' strengths and weaknesses in existing solutions, a competitive analysis was conducted. This helped guide the design to ensure it would be a standout user experience that truly meets the needs of students and advisors.

  • User Onboarding Effectiveness: Our analysis revealed variations in user registration and onboarding experiences across competing websites, highlighting areas of potential improvement.
  • Search and Navigation Efficiency: Our evaluation of competing platforms uncovered variances in user-friendliness, pinpointing potential usability advantages found within Uspire.
  • Advisor Matching Performance: An analysis of competitors' platforms revealed varying degrees of success in matching students with advisors based on their needs and goals.
  • Community and Social Integration Strategies: Our analysis examined how competitors foster community interaction and social integration among students and advisors.
  • Study Abroad Resources and Guidance Assessment: Our examination of competitive platforms scrutinized the availability and usefulness of study abroad resources and guidance.

    As a user, (counselor), I want to be able to accept requests from a student and schedule initial intro calls.

    As a user (counselor), I want to be able to send offers to the client (student).

    Reflection

    Designing this app held a deep personal significance for me because my brother-in-law has a disability in which he requires comprehensive support in his daily life. Because of this, I have witnessed firsthand the challenges and obstacles involved in accessing disability resources, particularly for adults. This drove my passion to assist the client in enhancing their existing website to better serve users.

    Embarking on this project, I found great excitement in my client's enthusiastic approach. It became clear to me that prioritizing the user's needs and ensuring the brand's identity aligned with its function were of utmost importance. Throughout the process, there were numerous obstacles, such as determining the right colors for the brand, which required multiple discussions with the client. However, the end result is a design that is inclusive and user-friendly, and it fills me with pride.

    Looking ahead, the design has the potential to expand as additional features, including a social platform, are incorporated into the website. I am eager to further refine and develop these ideas, building upon the foundation we have laid he final design of The Connected Parent platform offers an enhanced user experience, improved accessibility, and a refined visual identity. This project reinforced the importance of user-centered design principles in creating impactful digital solutions.
