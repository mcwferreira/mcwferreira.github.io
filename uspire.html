Overview

Uspire is dedicated to enhancing students' experience by optimizing the way they connect with advisors to help prepare with standardized exam preparation and preparation for studying abroad. With the goal of crafting an immersive and user-centric study abroad ecosystem, Uspire aims to create seamless digital experiences for students pursuing their academic goals worldwide.

Problem

Many students face challenges when seeking advisors who can provide tailored guidance for their unique circumstances while preparing for standardized exams. Additionally, students frequently encounter difficulties in locating advisors who can offer specialized support based on their individual needs. This often results in a lack of personalized guidance crucial for their exam preparation.

Solution

The team envisioned this product as a comprehensive platform where counselors and advisors can efficiently oversee and guide their students on personalized journeys to success. The team worked together to design the account creation, login, storefront, dashboard, and client request/offer screens to provide a transparent environment for managing resources and assessing each student's unique strengths and weaknesses. This resulted in a powerful tool that empowers counselors to effectively support and mentor their students, leading them toward academic achievement and fulfilling their dreams.

Discovery

Project Kick-off

Our team convened for a kickoff call to familiarize ourselves with the Uspire project scope. We reviewed a video showcasing the Uspire desktop app, a style guide, and competitor examples. We used this to ask questions and understand client goals, feature requirements, and design inspirations.

Client Goals

  • Minimalist Design: A strong emphasis on simplicity with inspirational brands shared by the client.
  • Advisor-Student Fit: Personalized advisor connections based on student needs.
  • Comprehensive Exam Prep: Holistic support beyond test readiness.

Competitive Analysis

  • User onboarding variations
  • Navigation and search efficiencies
  • Advisor match quality
  • Social and community integrations
  • Resource and content quality
Ideation

User Stories

  • As a counselor, I want to create an account and log in.
  • As a counselor, I want to showcase my services, reviews, and testimonials.
  • As a counselor, I want to accept requests and schedule intro calls.
  • As a counselor, I want to send offers to students.

User Flows

We mapped user flows based on the stories, particularly focusing on how counselors send offers. This visual guide supported efficient and intuitive experiences.

Medium Fidelity Wireframes

I created wireframes that clarified steps and interactions for offer-sending functionality. These mid-fis informed our UI decisions in the next phase.

Design

Inspiration & Exploration

We reviewed dashboards, notification systems, and calendar UIs across minimal, modern products to align with Uspire’s goals.

Style Guide & Components

We updated inaccessible color values while preserving brand integrity. We created a design system with a simplified color palette, iconography, and clean, readable typography. As a former landscape architect, I leaned into my experience building standards to develop a robust and scalable UI library.

UI Iterations

We explored different dashboard designs. The selected version was clear, concise, and fit the minimalist brief. The hi-fi designs built on this vision for user story 4, streamlining steps, emphasizing hierarchy, and aligning visually with the updated palette.

Developer Handoff

Each screen was thoroughly annotated to guide developers with exact dimensions, spacing, alignment, and behavioral instructions. This ensured a smooth and accurate transition from design to build.

Conclusion

This project emphasized the power of interdisciplinary collaboration. Working on an edtech tool that enhances access to meaningful guidance was both inspiring and personally fulfilling. I particularly valued the opportunity to create a consistent, inviting experience through a strong design system—drawing on lessons from my career shift into UX/UI from architecture.
